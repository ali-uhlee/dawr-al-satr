Overview

Dawr al-Satr means “Period of Concealment,” when the Imam remained hidden.
Leadership continued through trusted representatives.
The Imamate remained active, not absent.

Timeline

765 CE – Ja'far al-Sadiq passes away
Late 700s – Concealment begins
800s – Da'wa network expands
909 CE – Fatimid Caliphate established
